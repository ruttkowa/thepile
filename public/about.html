What it does

A card sorter that feels like holding the cards

Every card is shown at full size, perfectly readable — like holding a real card in your hand. Everything else stays out of the way until you need it.

Tinder-style swiping

Drag, flick, tap buttons, or use arrow keys. Right adds to your pile, left passes. Misswiped? Undo takes any number of steps back.

Scryfall-powered filters

Name, rules text, colors, rarity, set, format, price — or drop straight into raw Scryfall syntax. Presets like “Downshifted to common” get you swiping in one tap.

Multiple named piles

“Deck ideas”, “Trade binder”, “Maybe”. Switch the target pile mid-swipe, and re-swipe any pile later to cut it down or move cards between piles.

Export anywhere

Plain text, CSV, or JSON — default is one card name per line, ready for any deckbuilder. Pick exactly which attributes come along.

Private by design

No backend, no account, no analytics. Your piles and sessions live in your browser's storage and never leave your device.

Installable app

Add it to your home screen and it opens full-screen like a native app, with the interface and recent card images cached for shaky connections.

How it works

Three steps from “too many cards” to a tidy list

Filter

Pick a preset — the newest set, budget rares, fresh Pauper downshifts — or build your own filter, down to raw Scryfall queries like f:pauper o:"draw a card".

Swipe

Go through the results one full-size card at a time. Right = into the pile, left = pass, undo if your thumb was faster than your brain. Your spot is saved — leave and resume any time within a week.

Export

Copy or download your pile as text, CSV, or JSON. Re-swipe a pile any time for a second look, or split it into new piles.

Install

Add it to your home screen

The Pile is a Progressive Web App — install it once and it opens full-screen like a native app, with no app store required.

iPhone & iPad (Safari)

  1. Open The Pile in Safari — iOS only allows installing from Safari, not Chrome or other browsers.
  2. Tap the Share icon (square with an arrow pointing up) in the toolbar.
  3. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen.”
  4. Tap “Add” in the top-right corner.

The Pile now sits on your home screen and opens full-screen, no address bar.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open The Pile in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu icon in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap “Install app” (some versions show “Add to Home screen”).
  4. Confirm with “Install.”

Chrome may also show an install banner or icon in the address bar — either works.

Privacy

Just another frontend for Scryfall

  • No backend. The Pile is a static page; the only server it ever talks to is the Scryfall API for card data and images.
  • No account, no tracking. No sign-up, no cookies, no analytics, completely anonymous.
  • Your data stays yours. Piles and swipe sessions are stored in your browser. Sessions expire after 7 days of inactivity; piles stay until you delete them.
  • Open files, open format. Everything you keep can be exported as plain text, CSV, or JSON at any time.
